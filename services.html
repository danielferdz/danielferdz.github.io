Parasol Property Management has been established since 2006 and provides a range of services to support property owners in Los Alcazares and Roda Golf in the Mar Menor, Costa Calida area. We understand the importance of reliability and efficiency when owners need someone to look after their property whilst not living in the vicinity. We pride ourselves on our client’s satisfaction and offer friendly, approachable and affordable services whilst striving to maintain standards and in turn, appease our owner’s rental customers.

Key holding only

For our clients who do not regularly rent their property we can securely hold a set of keys to your property. If you have maintenance or renovations we can meet the relevant tradesperson at your property to provide access at an extra cost.
